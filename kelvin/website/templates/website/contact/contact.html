The most appropriate addresses for a number of common queries are below. The Ensemble also has a postbox in the University's Music Faculty.

  • To find out more about auditions and joining the ensemble, please contact stringmanager@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk for string players, or bwp@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk for brass, wind and percussion players.
  • For information on our friends and sponsorship schemes, please contact fundraising@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk
  • For other queries, please contact secretary@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk or webmaster@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk

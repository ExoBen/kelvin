The audition will consist of one prepared piece of your own choice, followed by an excerpt that is available below. Your chosen piece should be approximately 5 minutes long - the panel may stop you after this time.

Weekend auditions will take place in the University Music Department, 14 University Gardens - just along from the Queen Margaret Union on the 23rd and the 29th of September. Tuesday evening auditions will be in the Concert Hall, Main Building - A12 on the campus map, next to Hunter Halls on the 25th of September.

To book an audition please select a time below and complete the booking process. Can percussionists please book auditions on the Tuesday evening. If you are having any difficulties, please contact webmaster@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk.

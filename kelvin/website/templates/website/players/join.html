Kelvin Ensemble hold auditions shortly after term starts in September and October.

The Kelvin Ensemble runs auditions each academic year for its members. These can be students from any university in Glasgow. All players will play a piece of their choice which lasts about 4 to 5 minutes, and an excerpt that will be published on the website.

The audition will consist of one prepared piece of your own choice, followed by an excerpt that is available below. Your chosen piece should be approximately 3-5 minutes long - the panel may stop you after this time. Violinists wanting to audition for leader will also be asked to play a piece of sight reading.

Weekend auditions will take place in the University Music Department, 14 University Gardens - just along from the Queen Margaret Union on the 23rd, 29th and 30th of September.

To book an audition please select a time below, and complete the booking process. If you are having any difficulties, please contact webmaster@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk.
